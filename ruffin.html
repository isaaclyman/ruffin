Welcome to your new life.

Ruffin will help you go outside, meet people, and seize the day.

Don't worry about logging in. You can't. There are no passwords or accounts here.

Instead, you'll need three things:

A name (it doesn't have to be your real name).

A zip code, so we can match you with folks in your area.

One or two words describing a hobby—for example, "rock climbing" or "chess".
